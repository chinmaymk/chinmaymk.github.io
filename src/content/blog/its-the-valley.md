---

Six months since first stepping foot in the valley. Yes, it's called "the valley" — not Silicon Valley — and San Francisco is called "the city."

## Weather is awesome

Ample sunlight, enough cold, soothing breezes. Snowy mountains, the sea, and desert all within driving distance.

## Failure is a credential

Quite normal to have three or four failed attempts before success. It is almost a ritual to write detailed blog posts about failed products.

## Everybody writes code

Even marketing people know programming 101. My Uber driver asked questions about interfaces in Java.

## Everybody is killing it, all the time

In every coffee shop, intense discussions about disrupting industries. Geeks pitching to investors, Android study groups at dinner tables.

## Meeting legends is normal

Met Don Knuth, Peter Norvig, Vint Cerf, Andrew Ng. Awesome experience interacting with legends.
